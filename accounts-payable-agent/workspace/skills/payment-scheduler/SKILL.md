# Payment Scheduler

Schedules payments for optimal cash flow management.

## Capabilities

- Schedule payments based on terms
- Optimize for early payment discounts
- Coordinate with cash flow requirements
- Batch payments for efficiency
- Track scheduled vs paid

## Usage

Schedule:
- Upcoming payments
- Bulk payment runs
- Special payment requests

## Output Format

- Payment schedule (date, vendor, amount)
- Discount opportunities
- Cash flow impact
- Recommendations

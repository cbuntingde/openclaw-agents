# Case Law Researcher

Researches case law and legal precedents across jurisdictions.

## Capabilities

- Search federal and state case law
- Identify binding vs persuasive authority
- Verify case status (good law, overruled, distinguished)
- Extract holdings and key facts
- Compare cases across jurisdictions

## Usage

Provide:
- Legal issue or question
- Jurisdiction (federal, state, specific court)
- Time frame (recent cases, all time)
- Practice area or topic

## Output Format

- Case name and citation
- Court and date
- Key facts
- Legal holding
- Analysis of relevance to the issue
- Note on whether case is still good law

# Legal Document Analyzer

Analyzes and summarizes legal documents for key provisions and risks.

## Capabilities

- Analyze contracts for key terms
- Identify potentially problematic clauses
- Summarize legal documents
- Compare documents to standard templates
- Flag compliance issues

## Usage

Provide:
- Document type (contract, agreement, policy)
- Specific focus areas
- Standard terms to compare against

## Output Format

- Document summary
- Key terms and provisions
- Identified risks or concerns
- Recommendations for review
- Comparison to standard terms (if applicable)

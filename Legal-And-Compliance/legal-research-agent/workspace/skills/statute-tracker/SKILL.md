# Statute Tracker

Tracks regulatory changes and statutory developments.

## Capabilities

- Monitor federal regulations (CFR changes)
- Track state legislative activity
- Watch for new regulatory proposals
- Alert on compliance deadlines
- Track regulatory agency activities

## Usage

Specify:
- Jurisdictions to track
- Regulatory agencies of interest
- Practice areas
- Notification preferences

## Output Format

- Regulation/statute citation
- Summary of change
- Effective date
- Impact analysis
- Compliance requirements
- Related regulations

## Research Guidelines

When conducting legal research:
1. Identify the specific legal issue
2. Determine applicable jurisdictions
3. Search for binding authority first, then persuasive
4. Verify citations are current and good law
5. Provide comprehensive summary with proper citations
6. Note any contrary authority or split of authority

# Expiration Monitor

Monitors NDA expiration dates and manages renewal process.

## Capabilities

- Track all NDA expiration dates
- Alert on approaching expirations
- Manage renewal workflows
- Flag NDAs requiring action
- Maintain renewal calendar

## Alert Timeline

- 90 days: Initial awareness
- 60 days: Begin renewal discussions
- 30 days: Confirm renewal status
- 7 days: Urgent action required

## Usage

Monitor:
- Expiration dates
- Auto-renewal terms
- Renewal notification requirements

## Output Format

- NDAs expiring by timeframe
- Renewal status
- Required actions
- Calendar of upcoming expirations

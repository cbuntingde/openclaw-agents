# NDA Analyzer

Analyzes NDA terms and identifies potential risks.

## Capabilities

- Review NDA terms and conditions
- Identify concerning provisions
- Compare to standard templates
- Flag imbalance in one-way NDAs
- Highlight unusual or aggressive terms

## Usage

Provide:
- NDA document or text
- Specific concerns or focus areas

## Output Format

- Summary of key terms
- Risk assessment (low/medium/high)
- Flagged provisions with explanation
- Comparison to standard terms
- Recommendations for legal review

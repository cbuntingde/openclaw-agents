# Payment Followup

Sends payment follow-up communications.

## Capabilities

- Send payment reminders
- Track promised payments
- Document customer communications
- Escalate broken promises

## Usage

Follow up on:
- Overdue invoices
- Payment promises
- Customer inquiries

## Output Format

- Follow-up activities
- Response tracking
- Results summary

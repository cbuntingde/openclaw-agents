# Task Tracker Skill

Track onboarding task completion and send reminders.

## Onboarding Tasks

| Day | Task | Owner |
|-----|------|-------|
| Day -7 | Send welcome email | Onboarding |
| Day 0 | Provision accounts | IT |
| Day 1 | Equipment setup | IT |
| Day 1 | Team introduction | Manager |
| Day 3 | Complete HR paperwork | New Hire |
| Day 7 | Week 1 check-in | HR |
| Day 14 | Complete training | New Hire |
| Day 30 | 30-day review | Manager |

## Reminder Schedule

- 2 days before due: Initial reminder
- On due date: Final reminder
- 1 day overdue: Escalation

## Status Tracking

- Not Started
- In Progress
- Blocked
- Completed

# Welcome Sequencer Skill

Send personalized welcome emails and onboarding communications.

## Email Templates

### Welcome Email (Day -7)

```
Subject: Welcome to [COMPANY], [NAME]!

Hi [NAME],

We're excited to have you join [COMPANY]! Your start date is [DATE].

Here's what to expect:
- [DETAILS]

Best,
[HR TEAM]
```

### Day 1 Reminder

```
Subject: Your First Day at [COMPANY]!

Hi [NAME],

Today is your first day! Here's your schedule:
- [SCHEDULE]

See you soon!
```

## Notes

- Personalize all communications
- Include relevant resources
- Set clear expectations

# USER.md - Legal Research Agent

## User Profile

- **User**: Legal team members, paralegals, attorneys
- **Organization**: Law firm or corporate legal department
- **Experience Level**: Legal professionals

## Primary Use Cases

- Research case law for ongoing matters
- Track regulatory changes affecting the organization
- Analyze legal documents for due diligence
- Maintain awareness of relevant precedents
- Compile legal research summaries

## Preferences

- Detailed citations with pinpoint cites
- Summary of key holdings in addition to full case analysis
- Timeline of relevant legal developments
- Comparison across jurisdictions when relevant
